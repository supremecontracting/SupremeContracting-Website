Roofing Options

Specialty Roofs

Supreme Contracting specializes in many specialty roof systems. HandSplit & Resawn and TaperSawn shingles are the two Cedar Shake Shingle styles we offer. For slate shingle, we offer both synthetic and natural slate options.

Cedar

Handsplit & Resawn Cedar shake

HandSplit & Resawn cedar shake shingles have a rustic style appearance. These shingles are offered in different sizes to compliment any structure. The HandSplit & Resawn shingles have a minimum life expectancy of 15-30 years.

  • Boasting a rustic style appearance, these shingles add an elegant and timeless touch to any structure.
  • Available in different sizes, HandSplit & Resawn shingles can be tailored to complement a variety of architectural designs.
  • Beyond aesthetics, these shingles offer structural benefits with a minimum life expectancy ranging from 15 to 30 years.
  • Combining visual appeal with durability, these shingles provide a charming and long-lasting roofing solution.

Cedar

Tapersawn Cedar Shake

Tapersawn cedar shake shingles have a uniform appearance. These shingles are offered in different sizes and can compliment any structure. The tapersawn shingles have different life expectancies based on the thickness of the shingle.

  • Tapersawn shingles offer a consistent and uniform appearance, providing a sleek and modern aesthetic to any structure.
  • Available in various sizes, these shingles can be customized to suit the unique needs of different architectural designs.
  • The life expectancy of Tapersawn shingles varies based on thickness, allowing for a customized approach to durability.
  • With the ability to complement diverse structures, Tapersawn cedar shake shingles offer both aesthetic appeal and structural reliability.

Slate

Synthetic Slate

Synthetic Slate shingles are a look-a-like slate shingle made out of recycled rubber. These shingles are considered to be one of the few "green" materials offered. The life expectancy of these shingles is anywhere from 50-100 years.

  • Made from recycled rubber, Synthetic Slate shingles are environmentally friendly, making them a sustainable and "green" roofing option.
  • With a remarkable life expectancy ranging from 50 to 100 years, these shingles provide a durable and enduring solution for roofs.
  • Achieve the classic slate appearance without the weight or cost associated with natural slate, combining aesthetics with practicality.
  • Beyond their visual appeal, these shingles contribute to eco-conscious building practices, aligning with sustainable construction.

Slate

Natural Slate

Natural Slate is one of the longest lasting shingles available for roofs. Its beauty can bring any structure to life. These shingles can last anywhere from 50-125 years.

  • Natural Slate shingles bring an enduring and timeless beauty to structures, enhancing the overall aesthetic appeal of any roof.
  • Among the longest-lasting roofing materials, these shingles offer a lifespan ranging from 50 to 125 years.
  • Known for their premium quality, Natural Slate shingles provide not only visual elegance but also a high level of durability.
  • Choosing Natural Slate is an investment in a roof that not only stands the test of time but also elevates the value and aesthetics of the entire structure.

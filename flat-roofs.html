Roofing Options

Flat Roof Membranes

Supreme Contracting specializes in four different flat roof applications. The options below consist of Single-ply systems like TPO and EPDM, and Two-ply systems like Torch down membrane and Self-adhered membrane. Each membrane has a different life expectancy and application process.

TPO

Thermoplastic Polyolefin Roofs

TPO, Thermoplastic Polyolefin, is a rubber membrane made out of recycled rubber. TPO is mostly known for its commercial use, but can also be used for residential applications. TPO's life expectancy varies on the thickness of the membrane. The maximum life expectancy is 30-years.

  • TPO is a rubber membrane crafted from recycled rubber.
  • Primarily utilized in commercial settings, TPO can also find applications in residential projects.
  • The life expectancy of TPO depends on the thickness of the membrane.
  • The maximum lifespan of TPO is capped at 30 years.

EPDM

Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer Roofs

EPDM, Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer, is made out of a synthetic rubber. EPDM rubber is a very durable material for low slope applications. Just like TPO, the life expectancy of EPDM varies on the thickness of the membrane. EPDM has a maximum life expectancy of 50-years.

  • EPDM is a synthetic rubber material, renowned for its durability in low slope applications.
  • Similar to TPO, the lifespan of EPDM is influenced by the thickness of the membrane.
  • EPDM boasts a maximum life expectancy of 50 years.
  • Its composition, being ethylene propylene diene monomer, contributes to its robustness.

Torch Down Membrane

Torch Down Membrane Roofs

Torch down membrane, also known as modified bitumen, is a low slope membrane that is adhered through open-flame. The max life expectancy on this membrane is 20-years.

  • Torch down membrane, or modified bitumen, is a low slope roofing material.
  • The application involves adhering the membrane through an open flame.
  • The maximum life expectancy of torch down membrane is set at 20 years.
  • This type of membrane offers specific advantages but has a comparatively shorter lifespan.

Self-Adhered Membrane

Self-Adhered Membrane Roofs

Self-Adhered membrane is a low slope membrane made out of a modified bitumen material. This membrane comes with an adhesive that makes it one of the easiest applications. The life expectancy is 10-years.

  • A low slope membrane composed of modified bitumen material.
  • Distinguished by its user-friendly application, thanks to the built-in adhesive.
  • Considered one of the easiest roofing applications due to the adhesive feature.
  • The life expectancy of the self-adhered membrane is limited to 10 years.

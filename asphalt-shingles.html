Roofing Options

Asphalt Shingles

Supreme Contracting offers different brands and tiers of shingles that fit anyone's style and budget. Below you will find the difference in our GOOD, BETTER, and SUPREME options.

GOOD

Good Quality Roofing Materials

The "good" category provides a reliable, cost-effective roofing solution with the Landmark Architectural shingle (30-year). Using Tri-built Ice & Water shield and Tri-built synthetic underlayment ensures basic yet functional protection. Construction grade 3-in-1 pipe boot and Tri-built plastic ridgevent offer practicality for homeowners seeking a solid, moderately durable option.

  • Landmark Architectural shingle (30-year)
  • Tri-built Ice & Water shield
  • Tri-built synthetic underlayment
  • F5 Drip Edge along roof eaves
  • Tri-built starter shingles
  • Construction grade 3-in-1 pipe boot
  • Tri-built plastic ridgevent
  • Matching ridge cap

BETTER

Better Quality Roofing Materials

In the "better" category, upgrades enhance performance, introducing the Landmark Pro shingle (heavier architectural 40-year shingle). CertainTeed Winterguard Ice & Water Shield and Roof Runner synthetic underlayment provide superior weather protection. F5 Drip Edge, CertainTeed Swift Start shingle, and a higher-grade 3-in-1 pipe collar signify a commitment to longevity and improved performance.

  • Landmark Pro shingle (heavier architectural 40-year shingle)
  • CertainTeed Winterguard Ice & Water Shield
  • CertainTeed Roof Runner synthetic underlayment
  • F5 Drip Edge along perimeter of structure (eaves and rakes)
  • CertainTeed Swift Start shingle
  • Construction grade 3-in-1 pipe collar
  • CertainTeed plastic ridgevent
  • CertainTeed ridge cap

SUPREME

Supreme Quality Roofing Materials

The "supreme" category prioritizes luxury and maximum longevity with Belmont or Grand Manor shingles (Luxury 50-year shingle). CertainTeed Superior Ice & Water Shield and Diamond Deck synthetic underlayment offer advanced weather protection. F5 Drip Edge, high-performance starter shingle, and a lifetime pipe collar ensure top-tier longevity. The CertainTeed plastic ridgevent and high-performance ridge cap complete this premium roofing package.

  • Belmont or Grand Manor shingle (Luxury 50-year shingle)
  • CertainTeed Superior Ice & Water Shield
  • CertainTeed Diamond Deck synthetic underlayment
  • F5 Drip Edge along perimeter of structure (eaves and rakes)
  • CertainTeed high performance starter shingle
  • Lifetime pipe collar
  • CertainTeed plastic ridgevent
  • CertainTeed high performance ridge cap

Roofing Options

Asphalt Shingles

Supreme Contracting offers different brands and tiers of shingles that fit anyone's style and budget. Below you will find the difference in our GOOD, BETTER, and SUPREME options.

GOOD

Good Quality Roofing Materials

The "good" category offers a reliable and cost-effective roofing solution. Featuring the Landmark Architectural shingle with a 30-year lifespan, this category ensures durability for a reasonable investment. The use of generic Ice & Water shield and #15lb felt paper underlayment provides a basic yet functional protective layer against the elements. With Construction grade 3-in-1 pipe boot and generic plastic ridgevent, this category is designed for practicality, making it suitable for homeowners seeking a solid roofing option with a moderate lifespan.

  • Landmark Architectural shingle (30-year)
  • Generic Ice & Water shield
  • #15lb felt paper underlayment
  • F5 Drip Edge along roof eaves
  • Generic starter shingles
  • Construction grade 3-in-1 pipe boot
  • Generic plastic ridgevent
  • Generic ridge cap

BETTER

Better Quality Roofing Materials

Stepping up in quality, the "better" category introduces upgrades for enhanced performance. The Landmark Pro shingle, a heavier architectural 40-year shingle, offers increased durability. CertainTeed Winterguard Ice & Water Shield and Roof Runner synthetic underlayment provide superior weather protection. F5 Drip Edge along the perimeter, CertainTeed Swift Start shingle, and a higher grade 3-in-1 pipe collar signify a commitment to longevity and improved performance. This category is ideal for those seeking a balance between durability and affordability.

  • Landmark Pro shingle (heavier architectural 40-year shingle)
  • CertainTeed Winterguard Ice & Water Shield
  • CertainTeed Roof Runner synthetic underlayment
  • F5 Drip Edge along perimeter of structure (eaves and rakes)
  • CertainTeed Swift Start shingle
  • Construction grade 3-in-1 pipe collar
  • CertainTeed plastic ridgevent
  • CertainTeed ridge cap

SUPREME

Supreme Quality Roofing Materials

For those prioritizing luxury and maximum longevity, the "supreme" category stands out. Featuring Belmont or Grand Manor shingles with a luxurious 50-year lifespan, this category offers top-tier durability and aesthetics. CertainTeed Superior Ice & Water Shield and Diamond Deck synthetic underlayment provide advanced protection against harsh weather conditions. F5 Drip Edge, high-performance starter shingle, and a lifetime pipe collar ensure longevity and resilience. The CertainTeed plastic ridgevent and high-performance ridge cap complete this premium roofing package, making it an ideal choice for homeowners seeking the utmost in quality and longevity for their roof.

  • Belmont or Grand Manor shingle (Luxury 50-year shingle)
  • CertainTeed Superior Ice & Water Shield
  • CertainTeed Diamond Deck synthetic underlayment
  • F5 Drip Edge along perimeter of structure (eaves and rakes)
  • CertainTeed high performance starter shingle
  • Lifetime pipe collar
  • CertainTeed plastic ridgevent
  • CertainTeed high performance ridge cap
